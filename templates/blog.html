🌱 Branch Logic: Transforming Campus Recruitment One Click at a Time

In today’s competitive academic and professional landscape, finding the right job can feel like navigating a maze. That’s where Branch Logic comes in—a sleek, intelligent, and student-focused job portal that bridges the gap between opportunity and ambition.

🚀 What Is Branch Logic?

Branch Logic is a modern job portal tailored specifically for students and fresh graduates. Designed with simplicity, accessibility, and functionality in mind, it empowers users to:

  • Explore relevant job openings across departments
  • Upload and manage resumes easily
  • Apply directly to job listings with a single click
  • Track application status
  • Connect with top recruiters—all within a unified platform

✨ Why Branch Logic Stands Out

  • Role-Based Dashboards: Personalized views for students and admins with full feature access.
  • Smart Resume & Applicant Management: Admins can download resumes and Excel sheets efficiently.
  • Modern UI with a Glassy Touch: Sleek UI featuring a glassy navbar and a dark theme.
  • Secure and Efficient: Built with Flask, SQLAlchemy, Bootstrap, and more for safety and speed.

🌐 Features at a Glance

  • 🔍 Job filtering and searching by title, tags, or department
  • 📝 Resume upload and profile management
  • 📥 Excel export of applicants (per job or all)
  • 📊 Admin analytics dashboard
  • 📂 Job categorization by department
  • 🖼️ Responsive, accessible design

🎯 Who Is It For?

Branch Logic is designed for:

  • Students: seeking relevant job opportunities within their branch
  • Admins/Placement Officers: managing job drives and applicants
  • Institutions: digitizing their placement processes

🌟 The Vision

Branch Logic isn't just a project—it’s a movement toward decentralizing access to career opportunities. It ensures that every student, regardless of their academic branch, gets equal visibility and access to jobs tailored to their skills and interests.

Try Branch Logic. Because your future shouldn't be limited by your branch—it should be powered by it.
